# Spirits

Spirits are powerful companions that provide passive bonuses and unique skills to enhance your slayer's abilities. Each spirit has its own skill type and can be leveled up through 8 evolution stages.

## Available Spirits

There are **12 different spirits** in Slayer Legend, each with unique abilities:

1. **Sala** - Damage Over Time
2. **Ark** - Crowd Control
3. **Herh** - Cooldown Reduction
4. **Loar** - Boss Damage
5. **Mum** - Normal Monster Damage
6. **Todd** - Gold Farming
7. **Zappy** - Execute
8. **Radon** - Conditional Damage
9. **Bo** - HP Boost
10. **Luga** - EXP Farming
11. **Kart** - First Strike
12. **Noah** - Burst Damage

## Evolution System

Each spirit can evolve through **8 levels (0-7)**:

- **Level 0-2**: Early evolution stages
- **Level 3-5**: Mid-tier evolution
- **Level 6-7**: Fully evolved forms

As spirits evolve, they gain increased stats and their appearance changes to reflect their growing power.
