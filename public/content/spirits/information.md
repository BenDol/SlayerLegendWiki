## Evolution System

Each Spirit can be evolved through multiple levels (0-7), with each evolution level:
- Increasing skill power and effectiveness
- Improving skill duration or reducing cooldowns
- Enhancing the Spirit's visual appearance
- Unlocking stronger versions of their abilities

Evolution requires specific materials and resources obtained through gameplay.
